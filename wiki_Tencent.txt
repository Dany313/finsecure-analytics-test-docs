Known as Tencent Helix, it will accommodate more than 23,000 employees across nearly 500,000 square meters.\nTencent is the world's largest\nvideo game vendor\nand one of the\nlargest companies by market capitalization\nin China.\nIt is among the largest\nsocial media\n,\nventure capital\n, and\ninvestment corporations\n.\nIts services include social networks, music, web portals,\ne-commerce\n,
